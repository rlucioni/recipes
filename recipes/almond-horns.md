# Almond Horns

Mandelhörnchen in German.

## Ingredients

### Dough

- 300 grams almond flour
- 160 grams powdered sugar
- pinch of salt
- 2 egg whites
- 2 1/2 teaspoons almond extract

### Toppings

- sliced almonds
- 6 ounces chocolate

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
1. Put dough ingredients in a food processor and run until combined, scraping down halfway through.
2. Partition into 16 pieces. Roll each one into a tube, then bend into a horn shape.
3. Working one at a time, moisten top of each horn with water and press into a shallow bowl of sliced almonds. You might have to press more on by hand to get full coverage.
4. Bake until tan around the edges, about 13-14 minutes.
5. Cool completely. Melt chocolate, remembering to temper it by mixing in a few pieces of unmelted chocolate at the end. Dip each horn in the chocolate, letting excess drip back into the bowl.
