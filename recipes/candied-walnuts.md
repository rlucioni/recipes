# Candied Walnuts

## Ingredients

- 1 cup walnuts (or other nuts)
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 tablespoon hot water
- pinch of salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Spread nuts in a single layer on a lined baking sheet. Toast for 10 minutes.
2. Whisk sugar, water, and salt in a bowl until dissolved. Add nuts and stir to coat.
3. Return nuts to baking sheet. Toast until crisp and dry, about 12 minutes.
