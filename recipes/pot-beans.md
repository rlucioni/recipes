# Pot Beans

Adapted from [Rancho Gordo bean cooking tips](https://www.ranchogordo.com/blogs/recipes/cooking-basic-beans-in-the-rancho-gordo-manner)

## Ingredients

### Basics

- 1 pound dry beans, checked for debris and soaked 4-6 hours
- salt
- 2 bay leaves

### Optional additions

- 1 onion, chopped
- 4-5 carrots, chopped
- 4-5 celery stalks, chopped
- 2-3 garlic cloves, crushed
- kombu

## Instructions

1. If adding any optional aromatics, saute with olive oil and salt.
2. Add beans and enough water to cover by about 4 inches. Salt generously to season the beans and help them hold their shape, but be careful if you're planning to use the reduced cooking water later.
3. Add any of bay leaves, garlic, and/or kombu.
4. Bring to a rapid boil for 15 minutes.
5. Lower heat to a gentle simmer. Partially cover and cook until beans are done. This usually takes an extra 30-60 minutes depending on bean size and soak time. Check every 15 minutes or so to make sure the beans don't get too soft. If the cooking water starts to get low, add hot water (e.g., from a kettle).
