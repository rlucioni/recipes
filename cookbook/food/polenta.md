# Polenta

### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup ground corn
- 2 1/2 cups water
- 1 tablespoon butter
- salt to taste

### Instructions

1. Add ground corn and water to a pot. Bring to a simmer.
2. Cook at a low simmer. Stir frequently until polenta is soft and creamy, about 30-40 minutes.
3. Turn off heat and mix in butter and salt.
